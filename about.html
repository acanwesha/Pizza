WELCOME TO PIZZACO


PIZZACO is a fast casual, family owned & run pizza business located in Edmonton and St. Albert, Alberta. We operate in a quick service environment, with a focus on customer service and quality ingredients. All of that while creating a community around our pizza. We opened our first shop in the heart of Edmonton in January 2016 and we quickly became a regular stop for the noon lunch crowd, meeting up for pizza and drinks with friends in the evenings and gathering with family on Sundays. Our second location in St. Albert opened in April 2017 and have enjoyed becoming part of that community too.
